4.7.3 Document classification

In case of necessity of classification assignment to documents, the document defined in IfcDocumentInformation can be classified through the entity IfcRelAssociatesClassification and its attribute RelatingObjects (type of IfcDefinitionSelect). The IfcRelAssociatesClassification can be applied to the entire classification system or just to one item in the classification system through RelatingClassification (type of IfcClassificationSelect).
