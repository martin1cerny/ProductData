4.6.4 Document information

IfcDocumentInformation is the element adopted to digitize and point to external document metadata within IFC standard. It doesn’t provide details about the documentation’s content. The table below resume IfcDocumentInformation’s attributes and their optional/mandatory selection according to this standard:
