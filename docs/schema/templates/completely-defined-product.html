4.1.1 Completely defined product

Structure for (3) to be described here by Team 1.

The file for the completely defined products shall implement and embed the product data templates based on the definition in chapter 6.1.

In addition, the following classes from EN ISO 16739 shall be implemented to transport the product data itself:

  • IfcPropertySet
  • IfcSimpleProperty
  • IfcComplexProperty
  • IfcDocumentInformation
  • IfcDocumentReference
  • Occurrence objects derived from IfcProduct (IfcDoor, IfcWindow, IfcDamper, …)
  • IfcGroup (for assemblies)
  • Type objects (Matchcodes?)

One or more products that are fully defined and can be ordered, delivered to the site and installed into the building. These products are subsets of the completely defined product and shall be described with the same format, based on the appropriate LOIN for the project.
